## Why only now?

I've always dreamed about having a proper blog.

Of course; I do not just keep dreaming, I act!

So I start and lots of questions pop up.

- Where do I begin?
- What platform should I use?
- Should I use WordPress?
- Or should I build my own?

Heck yeah!
I'm programmer for Christ's sake.
Of course I'm going to build my own blog system; it's definitely going to be so much better than the 1000 other ones that I can use RIGHT NOW. 

Obviously, you know how that ends. You've been there yourself.

Another project bites the dust in a trash bin full of half-cooked Frankenstein monsters.

100th private repository on my GitHub that will never ever see daylight.

What's different this time?

## No longer perfectionist

Recently, I've watched a video by [Tom Scott](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h0AMaW4XRCI) and he shared this amazing graph:

Instantly I thought about my writing own blog.

Is it easy? Yep.

Is it awesome? Hell yeah.

Then something clicked in my head: starting a blog is so easy and awesome, why can't I just do it?

Everyone and their grandma has a blog. 
But not me?

Then I realised: **I am trying too hard.**

I just put too much effort into details that are not important.

I try to make everything perfect; however, that means that I must jump through hoops just to start the fricking blog.

Blog is all about the content.

It is not about cool looking SSR Vue.js application.
